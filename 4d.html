Welcome to 2x2 Chess

The rules are as follows:

  1. This is a two player game.
  2. You will play on both boards simultaneously.
  3. There is no 'check' warning, be vigilant.
  4. If you lose either king you lose the game.

Warping

  1. When a piece captures another piece, it gains a point.
  2. When a piece has 2 or more points, it may 'warp' to the opposite board as a move.
    (A piece will glow red when it can warp, click on the piece's tile on the opposite board to do so.)
  3. Any piece occupying a space will be captured when another piece warps to it.
